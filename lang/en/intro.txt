===== Recommend =====

Here you can create templates that will be used as placeholders in the email form.

The pattern can be one of the following:
  * Page ID
  * Namespace excluding subnamespaces are defined as ''<nowiki>namespace:*</nowiki>''
  * Namespace including subnamespaces are defined as ''<nowiki>namespace:**</nowiki>''
  * Regular expressions starting and ending with ''/'' are matched against a '':'' prefixed full page id
